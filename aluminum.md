# Aluminum (Al)

**Atomic number:** 13
**Atomic weight:** 26.98 g/mol
**Category:** Post-transition metal

Aluminum is the most abundant metal in Earth's crust, making up about 8% of its weight. Despite this, it was once considered more precious than gold due to the difficulty of refining it.

Key properties:

- Lightweight yet strong
- Highly corrosion-resistant
- Excellent electrical conductor
- 100% recyclable without loss of quality
